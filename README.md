t# Energy-Efficient-Fraud-Detection
FraudGuard-ML is a complete machine learning pipeline and dashboard prototype for energy-efficient credit card fraud detection. The system implements a Two-Stage Adaptive Architecture designed to minimise unnecessary computational load while maintaining high fraud detection accuracy.

The project was developed during a three-month Sustainable AI internship at Xellentro Consulting Services LLP, Sustainable AI Centre of Excellence, under the supervision of Faculty Mentor Dr. Jawahar S and Industry Mentor Mr. Sriram Narasimhan. The work was completed as part of Christ University Internship Cohort 1 by Team 6 (Joshua Kumar J, Anushaa Sri M, Harika C H, and Kanchi Agrawal).

The system consists of five integrated components: (1) a data preprocessing pipeline loading, cleaning, and SMOTE-balancing over 1.8 million records; (2) a feature engineering module deriving four fraud signals; (3) a two-stage adaptive classifier combining Logistic Regression (Stage 1) and XGBoost (Stage 2); (4) an energy proxy benchmarking module; and (5) an HTML/CSS/JavaScript dashboard prototype.
